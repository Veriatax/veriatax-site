Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24.02.2026

Veriatax Interactive (operated by Sven Martin Laschke, sole proprietor) develops and publishes mobile games and applications. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling data transparently and responsibly.

This privacy policy applies to our mobile applications and games. This website (veriatax.com) serves informational purposes only and does not collect personal data for analytics or advertising.

This privacy policy is available in German and English. In case of discrepancies, the German version shall prevail.

1. Information We Collect

Technical Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Performance diagnostics

Usage Data

  • Session duration
  • Gameplay events
  • Feature interactions
  • App activity

Advertising Data

Our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. The following data may be processed:

  • Google Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (country/region)
  • Device information

Ads may be personalized. Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) may be asked for consent before receiving personalized advertisements.

Users can reset or disable the Advertising ID and manage ad personalization settings within their Google account or device settings.

Analytics Services

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Remote Config
  • Google Play Games Services

2. Legal Basis (EU Users)

  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest)
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent, where required)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase
  • Google Play Games Services

Further information on how Google processes personal data can be found in Google's Privacy Policy.

External Fonts (Website)

For consistent display of fonts, this website uses Bunny Fonts, a service by BunnyWay d.o.o. Fonts are served from EU-based servers without tracking and without storing cookies. No personal data is transmitted to third parties through this service.

Hosting & Infrastructure (Website)

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and delivered via Cloudflare. When accessing this website, technical data such as IP address, browser type, and referrer URL are automatically processed by these services. This is technically required to ensure the security and reliability of the website (legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed on servers located outside the European Union in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes or as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under 13 unless explicitly stated.

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Requests can be sent to the contact email listed below.
