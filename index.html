About

The studio behind the pixels and beats

One Person. Many Passions.

Veriatax Interactive is an independent creative studio based in Germany, founded by Sven Laschke. What started as a game development project has grown into a multi-discipline brand spanning mobile games, electronic music production, and software tools.

Every game, every track, and every tool is crafted with attention to quality and a genuine love for building things. No corporate committee — just direct creator-to-user experiences.
